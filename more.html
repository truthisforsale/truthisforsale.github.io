All posters will be shipped unframed. Frames are not included in the price. For framing options please contact us.


Shipping

Free ground shipping (3-5 days) within the US. When it comes to International shipping, all posters are labeled as “Limited Edition Screen Printed Artwork” on the customs forms, but the full value is declared. This is necessary to protect myself as well as you, the buyer. Purchases exceeding $100.00 are insured at no cost to the buyer.


I placed my order four nanoseconds ago, how will I know when it ships?

Posters are usually shipped within 24 hours. When your order goes out, you will receive a confirmation email. Shipping time within the US is 3-5 days. Shipping time varies for international shipping.


How are your posters shipped?

Posters are being send rolled in a package of 5 x 5 inches. They are carefully rolled inside industrial grade craft paper. The craft paper roll with the poster inside of it is sealed with a piece of tape so there is extra room around the roll inside of the package.
