’Isaiah’ is a contemporary take on scripture. The work attempts to address current issues surrounding consumerism by using an astoundingly relevant verse from Isaiah's prophecies of judgment. It raises questions of consumerism as our new religion and responsibility, while looking to capitalism's crumbling promise of freedom and welfare. Ironically, the poster uses the language of sales and is itself for sale.
